# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024

## Overview

Behavior Tracker is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect any personal data.**

The app does not:
- Collect any personal information
- Track your usage
- Send any data to external servers
- Use analytics services
- Share data with third parties
- Require an account or login

## Data Storage

All data entered into Behavior Tracker is stored exclusively on your device using iOS Core Data framework.

Your data includes:
- Behavioral pattern entries
- Timestamps of logged events
- Optional notes and context you provide
- Your app preferences and settings

## Data Location

- **Local Storage**: All data is stored locally on your iOS device
- **iCloud (Optional)**: If you enable iCloud sync in iOS Settings, your data may sync across your Apple devices using your iCloud account
- **No Remote Servers**: The app does not use any remote servers or cloud services for data storage

## Data Access

Only you have access to your data through:
- The app interface on your device
- The export functionality (JSON/CSV formats)
- iOS backup systems (if enabled)

## Data Control

You maintain complete control over your data:
- **Export**: Export all your data at any time in JSON or CSV format
- **Delete**: Delete individual entries or all data through the app
- **Backup**: Your data is included in iOS device backups (if enabled)

## Data Sharing

The app provides functionality to export your data, but this is entirely under your control:
- Export creates a local file on your device
- You choose whether to share this file
- Sharing is done through standard iOS share functionality
- The app itself never transmits your data

## Notifications

If you enable daily reminders:
- Local notifications are scheduled on your device
- No data is sent to external notification services
- Notifications can be disabled at any time in Settings

## Permissions

The app may request the following iOS permissions:
- **Notifications**: For optional daily logging reminders (can be denied)

The app does NOT request:
- Location access
- Camera access
- Contacts access
- Any other device permissions

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in app updates. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

## Data Security

While the app stores data securely using iOS security features:
- Data is protected by your device passcode/biometric authentication
- Standard iOS encryption is applied
- No additional encryption is implemented within the app

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access all your data through the app interface
- Export your data at any time
- Delete your data at any time
- Discontinue use of the app

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services, including:
- No analytics services
- No advertising networks
- No crash reporting services
- No cloud storage services (except optional iCloud)

## Contact

This is an open-source project. For questions about privacy:
- Review the source code to verify privacy claims
- Check the app settings for data management options

## Compliance

This app is designed to comply with:
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

By design, compliance is straightforward as no data is collected or transmitted.

## Data Retention

- Data is retained on your device until you delete it
- Uninstalling the app removes all data unless backed up
- iOS backups may retain data according to your backup settings

## Summary

In the simplest terms:
- Your data stays on your device
- We don't see, collect, or transmit your data
- You have complete control
- No internet connection required
- Privacy by design

This privacy policy is accurate as of the last updated date and may be revised in future app updates.
